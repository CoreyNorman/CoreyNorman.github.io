About Us

Founded in 2024 by Katlyn Hickman, a dedicated Environmental Science student at the University of Phoenix, Plastic Free World is committed to eradicating plastic waste and promoting sustainable alternatives. Our journey began with a simple mission: to save our planet from the harmful effects of plastic pollution.

Our Mission

At Plastic Free World, our mission is to reduce plastic use by providing eco-friendly, sustainable alternatives. We aim to educate, inspire, and mobilize communities to join us in our fight against plastic pollution, protecting our environment for future generations.

Projects

Our projects range from local clean-up initiatives to partnering with businesses to reduce their plastic footprint. We're always looking for innovative ways to minimize plastic waste and promote sustainability.
